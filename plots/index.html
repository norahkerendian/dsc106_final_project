Normalized Mx for VR Shifting, Music 0.1Hz and Eyes Closed, Music Regular Over Time

Normalized CoPy for VR Shifting, Music 0.1Hz and Eyes Closed, Music Regular Over Time

→ Highly correlated with first plot
→ Forward-backward center of pressure

→ Even more stable


Normalized My for VR Shifting, Music 0.1Hz and Eyes Closed, Music Regular Over Time

→ Side-to-side rotation
→ No oscillation pattern, but more overall movement
→ More stable


Normalized CoPx for VR Shifting, Music 0.1Hz and Eyes Closed, Music Regular Over Time


→ Side-to-side center of pressure
